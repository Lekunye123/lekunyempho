Who Am I

I am a passionate and driven young woman with a strong commitment to
both personal and professional growth. At 22, I am already deeply immersed
in the world of technology, pursuing a degree in Information Technology and
specializing in Cybersecurity, an area I discovered as my true calling in 2024.
I am a quick learner, demonstrated by my dedication to earning 13 certifications
in Ethical Hacking and Penetration Testing, which highlights my proactive
approach to gaining knowledge and refining my skills.



My academic journey reflects a blend of creativity and technical exploration,
which aligns with my enthusiasm for solving problems and staying up-to-date
with industry trends. I have a strong sense of curiosity, always eager
to discover new concepts, whether in coding, reading, or exploring fresh ideas.



Outside of technology, I have a well-rounded personality. My interests in cooking,
traveling, and exploring new cultures show that I value life’s experiences and I am
open-minded. I approach life with a sense of adventure and a commitment to living
fully, both professionally and personally. I am someone who balances intellectual
growth with creativity, a love for learning, and a passion for making meaningful
contributions to the tech world.
